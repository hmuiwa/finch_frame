Logo Design

Haron Graphics Logo Design

This project features a modern and professional logo design for Haron Graphics. The centerpiece is a bold, stylized “HR” monogram enclosed in a partial circular form, rendered in vibrant blue with a slight embossed effect to add depth and texture. Complementing the icon is the brand name “Haron Graphics” in a clean, artistic typeface curved around the logo. This design emphasizes creativity and visual impact, ideal for branding across digital and print media.

Poster Design

Student Leadership Campaign Poster – Joy Ndanu

This poster was designed for a student leadership campaign under the Kitui County Universities and Colleges Students Association (KUCUSA). Featuring Joy Ndanu as a candidate for Kitui Central Representative, the design focuses on clarity, confidence, and campaign messaging. The layout uses bold color blocks—green, yellow, and white—to reflect trust, energy, and professionalism. Strategic use of visual hierarchy draws attention to the candidate’s name, slogan, and key values: Commitment, Innovation, and Leadership. The checkmark icon and vote call-to-action create a strong political branding element, while the urban skyline background adds a youthful, forward-thinking vibe. Ideal for both digital distribution and printed posters, this project demonstrates how effective design can support personal branding and civic engagement.

Social Media Post Design

Birthday Tribute Social Media Post Design

This vibrant and personalized birthday post design celebrates an individual's journey through the years. The layout features a circular collage of past portraits arranged in a dynamic radial format, highlighting key moments and expressions. At the center is a standout, full-color image of the celebrant, drawing attention with warm lighting and bold styling. Set against a soft golden background with glowing stars and a modern "Happy Birthday" greeting, the design blends festive warmth with a touch of elegance. Perfectly tailored for platforms like Instagram or Facebook, this project showcases a balanced use of composition, color harmony, and photo storytelling to create a visually engaging birthday tribute

Branding

Finch Frame Brand Identity – Mug Mockup

This project showcases the brand identity design for Finch Frame, a modern and dynamic company. The logo features a bold, stylized "F" combining deep navy blue and vibrant orange to convey trust, creativity, and forward-thinking. The mockup presents the brand applied to a ceramic mug, emphasizing real-world usability and brand cohesion. The use of color inside the mug and on the handle reinforces brand consistency, while the clean layout on a light wooden surface adds warmth and approachability to the presentation. This project reflects thoughtful brand execution, ensuring that the identity remains visually impactful across both digital and physical touchpoints.
